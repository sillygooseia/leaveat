How LeaveAt Works

No accounts. No apps. Build a schedule in your browser and share it with a link.

The basics — free for everyone

  1. 1
    person_add

    Add your team

    Open the scheduler and click Employees in the toolbar. Add each person's name, role, and a colour. That's it — no accounts, no email addresses.

  2. 2
    view_week

    Build your week

    Click the + button on any day column — or double-click anywhere in the grid — to add a shift. Set the employee, start time, end time, and an optional note. Drag shifts to move them; click to edit or delete.

  3. 3
    content_copy

    Copy a previous week (optional)

    Use the Copy menu to duplicate last week's schedule into the current week, copy shifts from one day to another, or clear everything and start fresh.

  4. 4
    share

    Share the link

    Click Share to generate a link. Anyone who opens it can view the schedule — no account needed. Free links last 7 days. The link is read-only; your original schedule stays in your browser.
