Privacy Policy

Last updated: {{ lastUpdated }}

LeaveAt is built around a simple principle: we collect as little data as possible. Because the app is local-first and accountless, most of your data never leaves your device. This policy explains exactly what we do and don't collect.

info

The short version: Your schedule lives in your browser. We only see a temporary, anonymised snapshot when you share a link — and we delete it when it expires. We never create accounts, track you across the web, or sell your data.

1. Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect or store:

  • Your name, email address, or any personal identifier
  • Your schedule data outside of the share-link flow described below
  • Cookies for tracking or advertising
  • Browsing behaviour, analytics events, or usage profiles
  • Device fingerprints or cross-site identifiers

2. Data Stored in Your Browser

All schedule data — employees, shifts, settings — is stored in your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly generate a share link or a Premium cloud backup. You can clear it at any time by clearing your browser's site data.

A small anonymous token is stored locally to associate your device with a Premium license if you purchase one. This token is not linked to your identity.

3. Share Links

When you click Share, a read-only snapshot of your current schedule is encrypted and uploaded to our servers. This snapshot:

  • Contains only the schedule data you chose to share (employee names, shifts, and dates)
  • Is not linked to any account or personal identifier
  • Is automatically deleted when the link expires (7 days for free; 30 days or custom for Premium)
  • Is accessible only to anyone who has the link — treat the link as a secret

We do not analyse, index, or retain the contents of share snapshots beyond their expiry.

4. Premium Cloud Backup

Premium users can optionally back up their schedules to our servers. Backups are:

  • Encrypted before leaving your device using a key tied to your license
  • Stored associated with an anonymous license identifier — not your name or email
  • Deleted when you request deletion or when your license lapses beyond the grace period

5. Payments

Premium purchases are processed by Stripe. LeaveAt never sees or stores your payment card details. Stripe's privacy policy governs how your payment information is handled. Please review it at stripe.com/privacy.

We receive only a confirmation of payment and a transaction ID. We do not receive your name or billing address from Stripe unless you contact us about a refund.

6. Server Logs

Our servers generate standard access logs (IP address, timestamp, request path, HTTP status code) for security and diagnostic purposes. These logs are retained for up to 30 days and are not used for advertising or profiling.

7. Cookies

LeaveAt does not use cookies for tracking or advertising. We do not use third-party analytics or ad networks. The only browser storage we use is localStorage on your own device for your schedule and license token.

8. Children's Privacy

LeaveAt is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has submitted data through the Service, please contact us and we will remove it promptly.

9. Your Rights

Because we don't hold personal accounts, most of your data is already fully under your control (it's in your browser). For any data we do hold — share snapshots or backups — you can:

  • Delete share links early from the share dialog in the scheduler
  • Delete cloud backups from the Premium settings panel
  • Contact us at support@leaveat.com to request deletion of any server-side data associated with your license token

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with a revised date. Continued use of the Service after updates constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. We will never materially change how we use your data without clearly communicating it here.

11. Contact

Questions or requests regarding your privacy? Email us at support@leaveat.com. We'll respond within one business day.
