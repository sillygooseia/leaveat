About LeaveAt

A simple scheduling tool built for people who just want to get on with it.

lightbulb

Why We Built This

Most scheduling tools are built for enterprise teams — loaded with features no small business or family will ever use, and guarded behind mandatory accounts and monthly fees.

We built LeaveAt because scheduling a small team should be as easy as writing on a whiteboard. No logins. No setup. Just open it, build your schedule, and share a link.

Our Principles

No Accounts Required

Your schedule lives in your browser. Nothing is tied to an account or email address.

Local-First

All data is stored locally. The cloud is only used when you choose to share.

Ephemeral by Design

Share links expire automatically. Nothing lingers unless you want it to.

Friendly, Not Corporate

This is a tool, not a system. We keep it approachable and free from bloat.
